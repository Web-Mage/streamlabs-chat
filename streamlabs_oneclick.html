Streamlabs Chat Box One Click Install

Last Update 01 May 2022
If you prefer manually installing the code, go to here instead.

Installation Steps

  1. Pick one of the themes from 'Themes' section below.
  2. Upon clicking on it, a new tab (Streamlabs webpage) will open. You have to choose where to install the chatbox. You can either use default/existing 'collection' or create new theme 'collection'. Choose your 'active' or your prefered collection.
  3. You will then be redirected to Streamlabs Chat Box widget page where all the settings and codes automaticly gets installed to.
  4. On this page, scroll down and click on Custom Fields section
  5. On this section, you can customize the chat box and make changes as you please.
  6. Lastly, you can then copy your widget url and use it on OBS.
